HOW TO ENROLL

Enrollment Procedures

Follow the appropriate enrollment procedure based on your admission category.

🧸 New Kindergarten

  1. See the Guidance-in-Charge for the Entrance Examination / Interview.
  2. Secure the Eligibility Form from the Principal's Office.
  3. Present your PSA Birth Certificate and Eligibility Form to the Registrar's Office. Secure and accomplish the Registration Form.
  4. Proceed to the Finance Office for Tuition Levelling.
  5. Pay the Enrollment Fee at the Cashier.
  6. Return to the Registrar's Office.

📚 New Grade 1 – Grade 12

  1. Present the admission requirements to the Registrar's Office.
  2. Secure and accomplish the Registration Form.
  3. Proceed to the Finance Office for Tuition Levelling.
  4. Pay the Enrollment Fee at the Cashier.
  5. Return to the Registrar's Office.

🎓 Continuing Students

  1. Present your original Report Card to the Registrar's Office.
  2. Secure and accomplish the Registration Form.
  3. Proceed to the Finance Office for Tuition Levelling.
  4. Pay the Enrollment Fee at the Cashier.
  5. Return to the Registrar's Office.
